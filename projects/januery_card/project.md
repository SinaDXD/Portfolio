# Januery Card

Source: https://sinadesign.ch/works/jlk-2025
Year: 2024
Collaboration: TRIBECRAFT
Services: Brand Identity, Digital Design, Printing
Duration: 8 Weeks

## Short description
A public-facing project from a nine-month full-time design internship at Tribecraft AG Zurich, centered on an interactive New Year card about repair and sustainability.

## Overview
Due to confidentiality agreements, only publicly released work can be showcased. One example is a holiday card designed for Tribecraft’s annual New Year tradition, known in Swiss culture as the Hole of January. Each card features a handwritten personal message and uses interaction to communicate cultural nuance and sustainability values.

## Challenge
The central concept of the card revolves around a literal and metaphorical hole: a symbol for a problem, gap, or loss. The challenge was to connect this idea to repair and sustainability while creating a poetic visual concept aligned with Tribecraft’s mission.

## Solution
The card comes with a red cord that wraps around and threads through holes labeled with words related to sustainability. The recipient follows the holes, opens the card, and gradually connects dots to reveal the word REPAIR, symbolically repairing the card through interaction.

## Result
The card transforms a simple printed object into an experience that invites reflection on repair, care, and sustainable action.
