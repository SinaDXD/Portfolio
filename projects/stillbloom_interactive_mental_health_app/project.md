# Stillbloom - Interactive Mental Health App

Source: https://sinadesign.ch/works/stillbloom
Year: 2025
Collaboration: Stillbloom
Services: UI/UX Design, Web Development, Digital Design
Duration: 8

## Short description
A speculative interactive object that visualizes mental energy.

## Overview
StillBloom is an ambient, flower-like artifact that expresses a user’s mental energy through subtle physical changes. Developed through digital and physical prototyping, the system connects a mental self-assessment app to a responsive servo-powered flower, creating a quiet, symbolic interface for emotional awareness and communication.

## Challenge
The project explores how we might visualize and share mental states in non-verbal, emotionally intelligent ways. The challenge was to translate abstract psychological input, like fatigue or energy levels, into a meaningful physical form that is intuitive, sensitive, technically feasible, and symbolically resonant.

## Solution
Using psychological research and user testing, the team developed a short self-check-in system inspired by healthcare assessment tools. Based on a percentage output, the flower opens or closes to represent energy levels. Users can also engage in a restorative ritual where the flower opens gradually during rest or meditation.

## Result
StillBloom acts as both a personal reflection tool and a social signal, bridging emotional awareness with speculative technology. It encourages mutual care and emotional literacy through narrative design, coding, sensors, and symbolic interaction.
